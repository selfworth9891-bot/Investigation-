  • Homepage: Clear value proposition (e.g., "Get the Truth. 100% Anonymous."). Keep it text-light with strong visual call-to-action (CTA) buttons.
  • Intake Form: A secure form where users submit targets' social media handles, phone numbers, or suspicious locations.
  • Pricing Page: Clear, tiered pricing options so users know exactly what they are paying for up-front.
  • How It Works: A brief 3-step explanation of your process to build immediate trust.
  • Client Portal: A temporary, secure dashboard where clients view updates and download your final report.

Step 2: Recommended Tech Stack

To keep your clients (and yourself) anonymous, use privacy-focused hosting and encrypted platform tools.

  • Domain & Hosting: Purchase your domain using Namecheap or Njalla (which allows crypto purchases) for maximum domain privacy. Host the site on WordPress.org for total design flexibility.
  • Secure Intake Forms: Use Proton Forms or Jotform Secure Forms to collect sensitive target details safely.
  • Encrypted Communication: Never use standard email. Direct your clients to download Signal or use an end-to-end encrypted email provider like ProtonMail for all case updates.

Step 3: Anonymous Payment Gateways

To allow clients to pay you anonymously over the line, you must offer both traditional and privacy-centric payment methods.

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Set up a Stripe or PayPal merchant account. Note: While the transaction shows up on their bank statement, you can set your business "Doing Business As" (DBA) billing descriptor to something generic (e.g., "DM Digital Consulting") so a partner won't recognize the charge.
  • Cryptocurrency: Integrate BTCPay Server or Coinbase Commerce. This allows users to pay via Bitcoin or Monero, offering 100% true anonymity for high-risk clients.

Step 4: Productized Investigation Services

Do not charge random hourly rates. Package your skills into clear, easy-to-understand digital products.

Package Name Deliverables Pricing Strategy
Digital Footprint Audit Deep-dive scan of hidden social media, dating apps (Tinder, Bumble), and forum profiles. Flat Rate (Low Tier)
Deep Background Check Public records, reverse phone lookups, asset checks, and known associates. Flat Rate (Mid Tier)
The Full Verdict Digital audit + active OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) monitoring + comprehensive PDF truth report. Premium Rate (High Tier)

Step 5: Legal and Liability Mitigation

Operating an investigation site requires strict legal guardrails to protect you from lawsuits.

  • Mandatory Terms of Service: Users must check a box confirming they legally own or have rights to the information they provide, and that they will not use your report for stalking or harassment.
  • Incorporate an LLC: Do not run this under your personal name. Form a single-member LLC through a service like Northwest Registered Agent to protect your personal assets.
  • Footer Disclaimer: Clearly state on your footer: "Not a licensed Private Investigator. All data gathered via publicly available open-source intelligence (OSINT) and digital methods."
